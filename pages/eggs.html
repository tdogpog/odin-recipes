Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1/4 cup distil white vinegar
  • 6 cup water
  • 8 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Combine salt, vinegar in large pot, bring to rolling boil
  2. Add eggs, reduce heat to gentle boil, cook 14 mins
  3. Remove from water, place into icebath, let cool for 15 minutes
  4. Enjoy for up to 1 week shelf life
